Travel Tips & Essentials

What to Pack

Light clothing for coastal areas, warm layers for safari mornings, comfortable walking shoes, sunscreen, hat, binoculars for wildlife viewing, and swimwear.

Weather Expectations

Coastal areas: Warm and humid year-round. Safari regions: Warm days, cool nights. Dry season (June-Oct) is best for wildlife viewing.

Visa & Requirements

Check visa requirements for Kenya and Tanzania. Ensure your passport is valid for at least 6 months. Vaccinations may be required for certain areas.
